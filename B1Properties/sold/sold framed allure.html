FRAMED ALLURE: A FUSION OF TIMELESS ELEGANCE & LOCAL ESSENCE

Designed by renowned Emre Arolat Architecture and CK Architecture Interiors, Framed Allure is a stunning three-storey villa that perfectly blends contemporary design with local cultural heritage. The villa's sleek, bespoke architecture creates a sophisticated atmosphere, while honoring the surrounding environment.

With meticulous attention to detail, Framed Allure offers a luxurious lifestyle through spacious, carefully crafted interiors that combine style and functionality. Every corner of the home reﬂects a commitment to quality and elegance, making it an extraordinary living experience for those seeking both modern comfort and cultural connection.
A true symbol of exclusivity, Framed Allure redeﬁnes what it means to live in harmony with design and heritage.
