Waterfront Luxury Meets Urban Convenience

Discover the ultimate blend of luxury, location, and lifestyle at District One Phase 3 Villas, situated in the heart of Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum City.

Just minutes from Downtown Dubai, DIFC, and Meydan, this prestigious gated community offers seamless connectivity via Al Khail Road and Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road.

Residents enjoy direct access to the world's largest 7km Crystal Lagoon — a serene, beachfront-style escape perfect for water sports, family activities, and relaxed city living.

The community features over 60% green open spaces, cycling/jogging tracks, lush parks, and top-tier schools/healthcare - making it an ideal address for families and investors.
