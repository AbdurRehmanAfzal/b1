Description

The Acres Estates by Meraas presents an exclusive collection of 5, 6, and 7-bedroom villas in the heart of Dubailand, where modern architecture harmonizes with natural elements of stone, wood, and glass.

These spacious retreats (6,913 to 11,089 sq.ft.) are enveloped in lush greenery and peaceful lagoons, offering the perfect balance of privacy, comfort, and connection to nature.

Enjoy seamless connectivity via Sheikh Zayed Bin Hamdan Al Nahyan Street and Emirates Road, with easy access to Dubai's key destinations. The community features swimmable lagoons, fitness centers, children's play areas, and wellness gardens.

With a flexible 65/35 payment plan and February 2028 handover, this is your opportunity to own a signature villa in one of Dubai's most prestigious neighborhoods.

Where Villas Grow from the Landscape

Welcome to The Acres, a brand-new community of standalone villas nestled within pristine natural surroundings, where modern living harmonizes with untouched beauty.

Serene gardens and azure lagoons grace this exclusive neighborhood, perfectly adapted to the undulating terrain and connected by meandering pathways that invite exploration at every turn.

At The Acres, indoor and outdoor living blend seamlessly - where every architectural detail brings you closer to nature, and every moment celebrates the ever-flourishing environment.

Nestled in Dubailand's most coveted location, The Acres offers an exclusive opportunity to own a villa that embodies timeless elegance and tranquil living. Choose between two distinct collections - Ivory and Amber - each masterfully crafted with unique design elements that celebrate natural beauty.
